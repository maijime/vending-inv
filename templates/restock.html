💡 How It Works

Restock Machine:

  • Enter the NEW inventory level after you restock
  • Items are automatically deducted from home inventory
  • Example: Machine has 2 Coke, you add 10 → Enter "12"

Add Home Inventory:

  • Record items you bought at the store
  • Quantities are ADDED to your current home stock
  • Example: Have 5 Coke at home, bought 20 → Enter "20"
